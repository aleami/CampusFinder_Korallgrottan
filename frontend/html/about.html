Features

Everything you need, nothing you don't

Built by students, for students. Every feature is shaped around real campus routines instead of generic campus tooling.

Interactive Map

Full KTH campus map with buildings, sections, microwaves, printers, restaurants, and fast marker navigation.

Study Room Booking

Find bookable rooms, check live availability, and jump directly to TimeEdit when you need a space fast.

Restaurants & Cafes

All 50 restaurant, café and kiosk spots in one flow, from quick snacks to lunch spots and student hangouts.

Smart Search

Forgiving search that understands shorthand and naming variations, so students do not need exact input.

Find Facilities

Locate microwaves, printers, and other campus essentials with dedicated markers and clean popups.

No Login Required

Open the site and go. The experience is fast, public, and optimized for real use between lectures.

How it works

Three steps, zero friction

CampusFinder is built to remove hesitation and make movement across campus feel immediate.

1

Open CampusFinder

No download, no login, no setup. Just open it and start moving.

2

Search or filter

Find a building, room, restaurant, or facility through the map and list flows.

3

Navigate instantly

Jump to the right place on the map and get the context you need without extra clicks.

4

Study, print, eat

Handle the practical side of campus life from one product instead of five scattered pages.
